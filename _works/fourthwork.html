Pulo do Lobo

The challenge on this project was to create a site for a product that could be bought online. That's where the Pulo do Lobo started.

Pulo do Lobo is an hipotetic olive oil brand from alentejo, which tells a story of a wolf and a waterfall and also how this waterfall allowed the production of a high quality olive oil.

For this the illustrations and also the design focus on telling a story from the product and the place from where it provains. Therefore, all the colors and contrasts where choosen in order to recall the portuguese olives.

The site was prototyiped in order to provide a simple user experience to the costumer, so he/she could get the best olive oil in an easy and online method.
